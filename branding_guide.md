# Krishi Mitra - Branding Guide

This guide establishes the visual identity, colors, typography, and styling guidelines for the **Krishi Mitra** application.

## 1. Visual Identity
* **Name**: Krishi Mitra (कृषि मित्र)
* **Tagline**: *"Empowering Farmers, Digitally."* (कृषकों का संबल, डिजिटल हल)
* **Core Concept**: Modern agri-tech merging tradition (leaf/farming) with digital assistance (the letter 'M' for Mitra).

## 2. Color Palette (Nature + Trust)
We use a clean, organic, and trust-evoking color palette to appeal to farming communities and agricultural stakeholders.

| Color Role | Color Name | Hex Code | Visual Sample | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary** | Dark Green | `#1B5E20` | `rgb(27, 94, 32)` | Represents lush crops, growth, stability, and trust. Used for headers, primary buttons, and branding. |
| **Secondary** | Light Green | `#4CAF50` | `rgb(76, 175, 80)` | Represents modern agricultural prosperity, technology, and health. Used for accents, badges, and positive statuses. |
| **Background** | Cream/White | `#F5F5F5` | `rgb(245, 245, 245)` | Soft off-white to reduce eye strain under sunlight. Used for page background. |
| **Surface** | White | `#FFFFFF` | `rgb(255, 255, 255)` | Pure white for cards, panels, and input fields. |
| **Typography** | Dark Gray | `#333333` | `rgb(51, 51, 51)` | High-contrast, soft gray for excellent readability. |
| **Accent/Warning** | Amber/Yellow | `#FFB300` | `rgb(255, 179, 0)` | Represents harvest, sun, and alerts. Used for warnings, weather indicators, and highlights. |

## 3. Typography
* **Primary Font**: *Outfit* or *Inter* (Clean sans-serif for numbers, text, and labels)
* **Secondary Font** (Optional, for headers/identity): *Playfair Display* or bold sans-serif
* **Hindi Font Support**: *Poppins* or *Noto Sans Devanagari* for localized content.

## 4. Logo Concept
The logo features an integrated Leaf and the letter 'M':
* A stylized leaf pointing upwards.
* The veins and curves of the leaf seamlessly form the letter **'M'** (representing *Mitra* - Friend).
* Color gradient: From Dark Green (`#1B5E20`) at the bottom to Light Green (`#4CAF50`) at the top.
